Mechanical Keyboards

The Mechanical Keyboard hobby is niche, driven by its dedicated community, which has seen fewer newcomers since the end of COVID-19. While some may view this hobby as obscure, it offers the opportunity to craft a keyboard tailored to your preferences. Embracing this pastime allows enthusiasts to delve into the intricacies of customization and create a typing experience uniquely suited to their tastes."

Keyboard Meetups all over the world.

At some keyboard meetups, people will also bring any spare keycaps, switches, or other keyboard accessories that they want to get rid of. Think of it as an in-person r/mechmarket where you can buy, sell, and trade anything mechanical keyboard-related. This also makes the process a lot safer as you know you will be getting the product for sure rather than having to wait for it to ship and run the risk of getting scammed.


Some meetups even host presentations on topics surrounding mechanical keyboards. They might cover things like soldering, how to design your own keycap set, how to design your own PCB, and much more. This is a great way to deepen your understanding of mechanical keyboards and even learn new skills as well!

Custom mechanical keyboards are like the Rolls Royce of typing experiences. Picture this: every keystroke is a symphony of tactile feedback, a satisfying click under your fingertips that resonates with each stroke. But it's not just about the sound and feel – it's about the precision, the control, the sheer joy of typing on something that feels uniquely yours.


And the best part? It's not just a hobby – it's a journey. Dive into the world of custom keyboard kits, DIY builds, and firmware customization, and you'll find yourself on a never-ending quest for the perfect layout, the ultimate typing experience. Each new keyboard is a blank canvas, waiting for you to unleash your creativity and make it your own.

Don't hesitate, give it a try. It’ll blow your mind.

But it's not just about performance – it's about immersion. Picture yourself lost in the rhythm of typing, the satisfying clack of each switch a symphony of productivity. With customizable layouts and programmable macros, you can streamline your workflow and take your productivity to the next level. Need to switch between applications with lightning speed? No problem. Want to automate repetitive tasks with the press of a button? Consider it done.


And let's not forget the aesthetics. Custom mechanical keyboards are a work of art, a testament to the beauty of form meeting function. From sleek, minimalist designs to bold, eye-catching colorways, there's a custom keyboard out there that's as unique as you are. And with customizable RGB lighting and artisanal keycaps, you can turn your keyboard into a true expression of your personality and style.
